About me

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate from the Department of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) at the University of Michigan, Ann Arbor (UM-Ann Arbor), fortunatately advised by Prof. Al-Thaddeus Avestruz. I received two M.S. degrees in ECE and Mathematics from UM-Ann Arbor. I received my B.S. degree in Electrical Engineering from Tsinghua University, Beijing, 2016.
